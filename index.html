Play Walking

The link can be any kind of element, but will need a data-yuckbox-song attribute in the form of a js object with at least an id. If id doesn't already exist in yuckbox, it will be appended to the playlist.

If you want your links to be playable without javascript, you could just use an ordinary a href="", while adding the data-yuckbox-song attribute.
